Alcohol Consumption
Data Exploration

Working with MySQL and Tableau, this project explored recorded data of alcohol consumption from the year 2011 to 2019. Data were collected from The Global Heath Observatory: Explore a World of Health Data, WHO.

Data source:
https://www.who.int/data/gho/data/indicators/indicator-details/GHO/alcohol-recorded-per-capita-(15-)-consumption-(in-litres-of-pure-alcohol)

Data Exporation in MySQL

Data were explored in MySQL by using skills like CTEs and funtions. For instance, we inquired into the locations that consume the most beer in the year 2019, wine consumption as a percentage of total consumption in Japan, and so forth.
See: alcohol_cons_DataExploration.sql

Visualization in Tableau

The visualization contains two dashboards: (1) View by Location, showing the alcohol consumption of different beverage types in the particular year by location and distribution of the most consumed beverage type in locations over the world; (2) View by Time, including, in the selected year, the ranking of the locations by the their consumption of particular beverage type, and the distribution of its consumption.
View on Tableau Public.
