Lexical Effect on Mandarin Chinese Onset Perception in Noise

A: “Hey, it’s time for lun–”
B: “lunCH? Come on, you’ve just finished your breakfast.”
However, what if the someone says “hey, it’s time for lunGE”?

By conducting identification tasks, this article investigates how lexically activated phoneme influences the perception of other phonemes that are actually presented, analyzing data with Multi-dimensional Scaling (MDS), Signal Detection Theory (SDT), Hierarchical Clustering (HC), and Max Entropy Grammar (MaxEnt).
